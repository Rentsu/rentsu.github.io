Who We Are

About Group 3

A team with a shared goal: learn by doing, and build things that matter.

Group 3 is a collaborative team of students focused on developing web and software projects using modern technologies.

We believe in learning through hands-on experience. Every project we build is a chance to sharpen our skills, challenge ourselves, and support each other.

From enrollment systems to POS applications and portfolio websites, we tackle real-world problems with practical solutions.

We're not just classmates — we're a crew that figures things out together.
